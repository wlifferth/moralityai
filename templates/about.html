TL;DR

Morality AI is a project that seeks to map out what human moral reasoning looks like. The goal is not to decide what's right or wrong, but rather to get a sense of how people speak when they're talking about their moral beliefs and values. Our artificial neural network reads any bit of text and then tries to figure out if it is meant with moral weight or not--we call this "moral saliency".

Read the paper!

This project was born out of my senior thesis; while a bit dry my paper, "Classifying Moral Sentiment with Deep Learning" fleshes out a framework for defining what a moral sentiment is, how to think about it both philosophically and linguistically, and the application of machine learning to the task.
